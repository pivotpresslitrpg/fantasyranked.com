Fantasy Ranked is live.

We cover the intersection of power fantasy, LitRPG, progression fiction, and harem fantasy — ranked by community data, updated weekly, and filtered by editorial judgment.

## What We Do

- **Weekly rankings** — updated rankings across power fantasy sub-genres
- **New release tracking** — what just dropped across LitRPG and harem fantasy
- **Cross-genre guides** — for readers who want to explore beyond their usual corner of the genre
- **Editorial commentary** — our take on what's worth reading right now

## Our Data

Rankings are built from community ratings on [LitRPGTools.com](https://litrpgtools.com) and [Harem-Lit.com](https://harem-lit.com). We combine both datasets and apply editorial curation to surface the best books across the full power fantasy spectrum.
